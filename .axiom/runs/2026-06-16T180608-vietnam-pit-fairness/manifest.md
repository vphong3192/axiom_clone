## Assumptions List

1. Vietnam average wage of ~7.7M VND/month (2024) is used as the benchmark for effective-rate comparisons at 1×/2×/5× average wage. This figure may have changed slightly by mid-2026.
2. The SI deduction base (10.5%) is applied uniformly in all effective-rate calculations. Actual SI may vary by salary cap; caps were not modeled.
3. Comparison countries' deduction structures for non-residents (especially Thailand) are not fully confirmed for all scenarios — flagged as honest gap in §2.1 and Phần 5.
4. The 1.7% PIT/GDP figure (World Bank 2018) is presented as potentially outdated; no confirmed 2023–2025 replacement figure was available during research.
5. Effective-rate comparisons are performed for a single earner with no dependents. Adding dependents would reduce the Vietnamese resident's effective rate further and widen the resident/non-resident gap.
