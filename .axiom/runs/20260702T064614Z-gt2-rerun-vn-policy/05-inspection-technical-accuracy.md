## Verdict: FIX-IT

The essay is otherwise the strongest possible version on this axis — every other load-bearing
number, date, tier and quote re-verified live, and all 16 pitfalls pass. But one issue meets
the automatic-FIX-IT bar and it is the precise hole this inspection exists to close:

**Required fix (single, narrow):** the fiscal-autonomy counts 17/63 (2025) and 7/34 (2026).
Do exactly one of:
  1. Verify both counts against the primary appendices of NQ 160/2024/QH15 and NQ 246/2025/QH15
     (or a source that reproduces them) and cite it — then [B]/[A] as warranted; or
  2. Downgrade to [C] and soften to a non-exact statement (e.g. "một số ước tính cho thấy tỷ
     lệ tỉnh tự cân đối ngân sách giảm sau sáp nhập, chưa đối chiếu được toàn văn phụ lục"); or
  3. Drop the exact fractions and keep only the qualitative point.
  In whichever case, use a single consistent metric label for both years (either "có điều tiết
  về TW" or "tự cân đối hoàn toàn", not one for each) and tone down "đúng như luận điểm của bài"
  so a B/C figure is not presented as confirmation of the thesis.
